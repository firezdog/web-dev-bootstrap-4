李白《静夜思》
Li Bai
"Longing In The Dead Of Night"

床前明月光，
Bright moonlight by the bed
疑是地上霜。
Seems like frost on the ground
举头望明月，
I lift my head to watch the bright moon
低头思故乡。
I drop my head and long for my home-town.

王之涣《登鹳雀楼》
Wang Zhihuan
"Climbing Heron Tower"

白日依山尽，
The bright sun saturates the mountain
黄河入海流。
The yellow river flows into the sea
欲穷千里目，
If you want a thousand mile vista
更上一层楼。
Ascend one more floor.

白居易《草》
Bai Juyi
"Grass"

离离原上草，
From the beginning of time grass on the plain
一岁一枯荣。
A new stalk sprouting every year.
野火烧不尽，
If the field caught fire it would burn to no end,
春风吹又生。
But rise again in the spring wind.
